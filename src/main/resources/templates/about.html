About Us

Mission, Vision and Values

Mission Statement

To enrich lives and communities through universal access to knowledge lifelong learning, and literacy.

Vision

To develop strong library branches that are vital community destinations for knowledge, inspiration, innovation, and renewal.

Values

Leadership and Innovation: Provide excellence in library services through open communication, team work and cooperation, respect and recognition, education and learning, and by incorporating innovative solutions in a quality library environment.

Information and Literacy: We believe in the importance of informed and literate communities, and we provide services and programs to support lifelong learning.

Tradition and Technology: We value the tradition of the library and the legacy of the printed word, and embrace the benefits that modern technology provides.
